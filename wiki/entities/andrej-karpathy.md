# Andrej Karpathy

> Computer scientist, ML researcher, and educator. Originator of the LLM Wiki pattern this project's `wiki/` directory implements.

## Role in this wiki

Karpathy authored the seminal LLM Wiki gist (Apr 2026) that defines the 3-layer architecture (raw / wiki / schema) + 3 operations (ingest / query / lint) + index/log convention. His framing of "the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase" is the design contract LoverClinic's wiki follows.

## Cited works in this wiki

- [LLM Wiki gist](../sources/karpathy-llm-wiki.md) — gist `442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f`. The seminal source.

## Relevant background (publicly known, not from any source ingested here)

- Former Director of AI at Tesla
- Co-founder of OpenAI (early team)
- Stanford CS231n instructor — popularized deep-learning education
- "Software 1.0 / 2.0 / 3.0" framing (3.0 = natural-language programming; LLM Wiki is a 3.0-flavored knowledge tool)

## Why he matters here

The LLM Wiki pattern is not unique to him in spirit — it traces back to Vannevar Bush's 1945 Memex — but Karpathy provided the practical recipe (3-layer + 3-op + index/log) and the framing that makes LLMs the right "operator" for it. Before this gist, RAG-on-raw-docs was the dominant pattern; the gist articulates why a maintained-by-LLM markdown wiki beats RAG for sustained personal knowledge bases.
